What data we collect and why

Data you provide voluntarily

When you contact via email (contact@davidalvarezp.com), the following data is processed:

  • Email address and name (as included in your message)
  • Content of your message
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6.1.f GDPR) in responding to professional inquiries.
Retention: Email correspondence is retained for up to 2 years, or for the duration of any professional engagement plus 1 year.

Technical and browsing data

When you visit this website, standard server logs may record:

  • IP address (anonymised at log level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and timestamps
  • Referring URL

Legal basis: Legitimate interest in maintaining the security and proper functioning of the website.
Retention: Server logs are retained for 30 days maximum.

Cookies and local storage

This website uses minimal local storage entries for theme preference and cookie consent state. Analytics cookies are only activated with your explicit consent. See the Cookie Policy for full details.

Third-party services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (Google LLC), which may involve the transfer of your IP address to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.

No advertising networks, tracking pixels, social media trackers, or data brokers are used on this website.

External links (GitHub, LinkedIn, YouTube) lead to third-party services with their own privacy policies. The owner of this site has no control over those services.

International transfers

Google Fonts may involve data transfers to the United States. Google LLC adheres to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, providing adequate protection under GDPR Article 45.

No other international transfers of personal data are made by this website.

Your rights

Under the GDPR and LOPDGDD, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of them, contact: contact@davidalvarezp.com. Requests will be responded to within 30 days.

Access (Art. 15)
Request confirmation of whether your data is being processed and obtain a copy.
Rectification (Art. 16)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
Erasure (Art. 17)
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention obligations.
Restriction (Art. 18)
Request that processing of your data be restricted under certain conditions.
Portability (Art. 20)
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Objection (Art. 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD): www.aepd.es

Security

This website is served over HTTPS with HSTS headers. The server is hardened with industry-standard security practices. However, no system is 100% secure. If you have security concerns about this website, please contact contact@davidalvarezp.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in legal requirements or website functionality. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
