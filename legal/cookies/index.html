Cookie Policy

LSSI-CE · GDPR/RGPD · Last updated: March 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They are used to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and provide functionality. This website uses localStorage entries rather than traditional HTTP cookies for site preferences, and only loads analytics tools with your explicit consent.

Cookies and storage we use

Name Type Purpose Duration Provider
dap-theme Necessary Stores your dark/light theme preference so it persists across visits. Persistent (localStorage) davidalvarezp.com
dap-cookies-consent Necessary Stores your cookie consent choices so the banner doesn't reappear on every visit. Persistent (localStorage) davidalvarezp.com
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics 4 cookies used to measure visitor count, pages viewed, and traffic sources. IP is anonymised. Only loaded if you consent to analytics cookies. 1 year Google LLC
Google Fonts request Third-party Loading fonts from fonts.googleapis.com sends your IP address to Google servers. No cookie is set, but the request itself may be logged by Google. Session Google LLC

Note: Analytics cookies are currently not active on this site. They are listed for transparency in case they are activated in the future, and will only ever be loaded with your prior consent.

Legal basis

  • Necessary cookies — processed under the legitimate interest basis (Article 6.1.f GDPR) as they are essential to the basic functioning of the site. No consent required.
  • Analytics cookies — processed only on the basis of your freely given, specific, and informed consent (Article 6.1.a GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time.
