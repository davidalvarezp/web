Who I Am

I'm a Systems Administrator specialized in Cybersecurity. My technical foundation covers the full spectrum of server management — from configuring bare-metal Linux systems and containerized infrastructures, to designing and auditing secure network architectures.

On the security side, I hold certifications from Cisco in Junior Cybersecurity Analysis Career and Ethical Hacking, and my hands-on experience includes vulnerability scanning, penetration testing methodologies, threat monitoring, and access control policy design.

I also develop and maintain web infrastructure: I've built production platforms using WordPress, PHP, and Hugo, with a strong focus on performance, SEO, and security hardening at every layer.

My philosophy is simple: "making things work without anyone noticing". Good infrastructure is invisible — it just works, is secure by default, and scales gracefully. I'm driven by anticipating failure modes before they happen and building systems that are resilient, auditable, and maintainable by anyone on the team.

Beyond purely technical work, I've run digital strategy and content production — managing live streaming setups, video editing pipelines, SEO campaigns, and performance analytics.

Currently open to new projects in cybersecurity consulting, infrastructure administration, and secure web development.
